Detailed Description

Stores "inner angles" between star triples.

Unsensitive to first-order error in basic camera parameters (eg, wrong FOV or principal point), can be sensitive to second-order errors (eg, camera distortion, which may cause the effective FOV or principal point to be different in different parts of the image). Used for Mortari's Non-Dimensional Star-ID
